Give me an example of when you showed initiative and took the lead / 你有没有主动请缨牵头做过任务？

Give me an example of a time when you motivated others / 你是如何鼓舞团队的？

Tell me about a time when you delegated a project effectively / 你是如何给团队有效的分配任务的？

Can you tell me about your most recent experience working with a team? What was your role? How did you interact with the other team members? / 跟我讲一个你最近一次团队经历。你在团队里的角色是什么？你是怎么跟其他团队成员合作的？

Can you describe a time when you tried to solve a dispute within the team? What was the result? /你有没有在团队中是解决过纷争？你是如何做的？结果如何？

Can you describe a time when a team member wasn’t doing their work? What did you do? / 有没有过团队成员不干事的经历？你是怎么处理的？

Tell me about a time when you had too many things to do and you were required to prioritize your tasks / 当任务很多的时候，你是如何处理的？

Describe a long-term project that you managed. How did you keep everything moving along in a timely manner? / 当你执行一个长期的任务的时候，你是如何保证任务按时推进的？

Sometimes it’s just not possible to get everything on your to-do list done. Tell me about a time your responsibilities got a little overwhelming. What did you do? / 当你的任务实在太多，根本就无法处理完时，你是怎么处理的？

Describe a situation in which you were able to use persuasion to successfully convince someone to see things your way /Describe a situation in which you were able to use persuasion to successfully convince someone to see things your way / 给我讲一个你成功说服他人接受你的思路的例子

Tell me about a successful presentation you gave and why you think it was a hit / 给我讲一个你做过的成功的演讲。为什么你觉得这个演讲很成功？

Tell me about a time when you had to rely on written communication to get your ideas across to your team / 你有没有过必须通过写作来阐述你观点的经历？你是如何做的？

Give me an example of a time when you had to explain something fairly complex to a frustrated client. How did you handle this delicate situation? /你有没有过需要给人解释很复杂的概念的经历？你是怎么处理的？
